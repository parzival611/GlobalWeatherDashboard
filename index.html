WeatherPY


The purpose of this project, titled "WeatherPy", was to use Python to determine the relationship between weather and latitude. It is well known that temperatures increase as you approach the equator but can we prove it? By pulling real-time weather information from hundreds of positions around the globe and plotting the information vs its latitude we are able to draw some conclusions.

Here we used the API from https://openweathermap.org to get real time weather information.
